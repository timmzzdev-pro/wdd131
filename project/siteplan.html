Miracle Pavilion Church

Your Place of Worship, Growth, and Community

Site Name

Miracle Pavilion Church

This name represents a place of divine encounter, miracles, and spiritual growth. It reflects the mission of the church to be a pavilion where lives are transformed.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an online presence for Miracle Pavilion Church. It will provide information about church services, weekly activities, sermons, events, and directions. It will also help new members connect with the church.

Scenarios

  • What time does Sunday service and weekly activities hold?
  • Where is the church located and how can I get directions?
  • How can I join or contact the church?

Color Schema

  • Blue (#1E3A8A): Used for headers and navigation
  • Green (#16A34A): Used for buttons and highlights
  • White (#FFFFFF): Background color

Typography

  • Arial: Body text
  • Georgia: Headings
