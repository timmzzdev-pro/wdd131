Miracle Pavilion Church

Your Place of Worship, Growth, and Community

Site Name

Miracle Pavilion Church

This name reflects a spiritual place where miracles happen and people grow in faith. It represents a welcoming church environment focused on transformation and community.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as the official online platform for Miracle Pavilion Church. It will provide information about Sunday services, weekly activities, vigils, announcements, and directions to the church location. It will also allow visitors and new members to learn about the church and get connected.

Scenarios

  • What time does Sunday service, Tuesday, and Thursday activities hold?
  • Where is the church located and how can I get directions?
  • What programs like vigils or special events are available?

Color Schema

The website will use the following colors consistently:

  • Blue (#1E3A8A): Used for headings, header background, and navigation areas.
  • Green (#16A34A): Used for buttons, footer, and highlights.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Used as the main background color.

Typography

  • Georgia: Used for all headings (h1, h2) to give a formal and elegant look.
  • Arial: Used for body text for readability.
